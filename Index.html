About Me

Hi, I am Raf!

I spend most of my days working as an electrical engineer. I am also fueled with the passion of helping students in math and science. I have a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the Wentworth Institute of Technology, and a Master of Science in Electrical Engineering from the University of Rhode Island.

Started tutoring in community college in 2016. I would tutor students in pre-algebra, algebra and calculus. Tutoring in the environment was awesome, because I got to teach different students of different ages and levels. And providing them with individualized approached contributed to their success. I focus on identifying their strengths and weaknesses. I always try to make it fun and add real world applications to the sessions.

I was also a teacher assistant in lab coures. I thought students to program Arduinos! Ask more if interested!
